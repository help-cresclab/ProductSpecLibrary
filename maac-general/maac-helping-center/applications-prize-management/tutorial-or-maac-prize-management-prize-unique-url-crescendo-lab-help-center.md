* Optimize experience for redemption

## Five Key Benefits of Prize Unique URL

* Diverse and Flexible Prize Management\
  Brands can integrate third-party vendor coupons, configure coupons through MAAC, and offer contacts a more diverse and flexible range of prizes.
* Time-Efficient Coupon Setup\
  Brands can upload prize unique URLs in bulk, saving time in the prize setup process.
* Prize Security\
  Brands can upload prize unique URLs with redemption codes in bulk, enhancing the security of the prizes.
* Optimize experience for redemption\
  Contacts can directly claim prizes through the LINE official account, achieving a one-click redemption experience.
* Comprehensive Individual Tracking\
  Through exclusive coupon links, brands can comprehensively track the usage status of prizes.

![Click on Coupon](<../../../.gitbook/assets/d89a7d37 9d4f 4001 80ef 5d4651288ddf.png>)

### Redemption method — choose "Unique URL" or "Unique URL and code"

* Unique URL: Contacts can open the third-party page for prize redemption without requiring a password.
* Unique URL and Serial Number: Provide contacts with the serial number (verification code) for claiming the prize on the third-party page after opening the link.
