# Why there are no friends in the MAAC when I finish the connection ? – Crescendo Lab Help Center

There are no friends in MAAC because we have not imported your LINE friends' information into your database yet. There are several ways to import your LINE friends' data. Please refer to the instructions below.

Import methods

* LINE Verified Account (Blue Shield) or Premium Account (Green Shield)
  * You can use the LINE API to import all the friends' data.
  * Settings: Enter MAAC platform > Audience > Member > Import/export > Import list > LINE API > Continue to import\
    (The time to import the list depends on the number of friends. You will receive a notification at the bell in the upper right corner after the import is completed.)
* General unverified accounts (Grey Shield)
  * You need to let friends click the tracelink naturally to import friends from the LINE OA platform to MAAC.

Other options

* In addition to importing friends' information using the LINE API, here are other ways for your reference:
  * For new friends: create the "Deeplink" to let new friends join your official account through the link.
  * For existing/old friends: create the "Tracelink" and put it into a broadcast from MAAC. After friends click the link, their information will be imported into MAAC.
