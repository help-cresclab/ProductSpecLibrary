# \[Internal] EDM in Journey Onboarding SOP

**\[ZH] EDM Onboarding SOP**

## EDN in Journey 功能啟用流程

#### ![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/Unknown image (1)>)

#### Step 1：客戶提交申請

客戶自行於 [幫助中心填寫申請表單，發起開通請求。](https://crescendolab.zendesk.com/hc/zh-tw/articles/53570915864089-%E8%A8%AD%E5%AE%9A%E6%95%99%E5%AD%B8-%E5%A6%82%E4%BD%95%E5%95%9F%E7%94%A8-Email-%E6%B8%A0%E9%81%93%E4%B8%A6%E5%8C%AF%E5%85%A5%E8%81%AF%E7%B5%A1%E4%BA%BA#h_01KCVTRNRMJR0G5C53NBPB68JQ)

#### Step 2：PO 提供 DNS 資料

PO 收到申請通知後，產出 DNS 設定檔 (Google Sheet) 並透過 Asana 提供給 CSM。(時程：5 個工作天)

#### Step 3：CSM 寄送 DNS 指引

CSM 使用 Email 範本，將 DNS 資料寄送給客戶。 _註： (c.c.)_[_product.ops@cresclab.com_](mailto:product.ops@cresclab.com)

#### Step 4：客戶設定 DNS

客戶完成網域的 DNS 登錄，並回信通知 CSM 與 PO：「已完成登錄 DNS」。

####

#### Step 5：PO 驗證設定

PO 於系統驗證 DNS 設定無誤(時程：1 個工作天內)，並通知 CSM 回覆客戶 (驗證通過，待開通渠道)。

#### Step 6：PO 開通服務

PO 完成 Email Channel 建立，並確認服務已成功啟用。(時程：5 個工作天內)

#### Step 7：CSM 請客戶準備暖身

CSM 回信通知開通完成，並請客戶開始準備 Warm-up 作業 (匯入 Email 聯絡人 + 建立 EDM 範本)。

#### Step 8：客戶完成準備

客戶完成 Warm-up 所需的前置作業 (匯入 Email 聯絡人 + 建立 EDM 範本)，並回信通知團隊。

#### Step 9：CSM 回信 warm-up 開始

待 PO 驗證資料無誤後，CSM 通知客戶：「漸強將開始進行 14 天 Warm-up 流程」(將代為分批發送暖身信)。

#### Step 10：ENG\&PO 執行 warm-up

PO 通知 ENG 團隊針對該客戶執行 Internal Warm-up API，為期 14 天。

#### Step 11：CSM 通知顧客已完成

CSM 通知客戶 Warm-up 已結束，可開始正常使用 EDM in Journey 功能。 _註：若 Warm-up 期間垃圾信比例 (Spam rate) 過高，客戶須清洗名單，流程將退回至 Step 7。_

**\[EN] EDM Onboarding SOP**

## EDN in Journey Onboarding

#### ![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/Unknown image (1)>)

#### Step 1: [Customer Submits Application](https://tally.so/r/81Z4El)

The customer fills out the application form via the [Help Center](https://crescendolab.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/53570915864089-Onboarding-Guide-How-to-Enable-Email-Channels-and-Import-Contacts#h_01KCVTRNRMJR0G5C53NBPB68JQ) to initiate the request on their own.

#### Step 2: PO Provides DNS Data

Upon receiving the application notification, the PO generates the DNS configuration data (Google Sheet) and shares it with the CSM via Asana. _(Timeline: 5 business days)_

#### Step 3: CSM Sends DNS Instructions

The CSM emails the DNS data to the customer using the standard template. _Note: Please c.c._[_product.ops@cresclab.com_](mailto:product.ops@cresclab.com)

#### Step 4: Customer Configures DNS

The customer completes the DNS registration on their domain and replies to the CSM & PO to confirm: "DNS registration completed."

#### Step 5: PO Verifies Configuration

The PO verifies the correctness of the DNS setup in the system _(Timeline: Within 1 business day)_ and informs the CSM to reply to the customer (Verification passed, pending channel activation).

#### Step 6: PO Activates Email Channel

The PO completes the creation of the Email Channel and confirms that the service has been successfully enabled. _(Timeline: Within 5 business days)_

#### Step 7: CSM Requests Warm-up Prep

The CSM replies to notify the customer that activation is complete and requests them to prepare for the warm-up phase (Import email contacts + Create an EDM template).

#### Step 8: Customer Finalizes Preparation

The customer completes the required warm-up preparations and replies to notify the team.

#### Step 9: CSM announces Warm-up

After the PO verifies the customer's data, CSM informs the customer: "Crescendo Lab will now begin the 14-day warm-up process" (sending batches on the customer's behalf).

#### Step 10: ENG Executes Warm-up

The PO notifies the ENG team to execute the internal warm-up API for the customer for a duration of 14 days.

#### Step 11: CSM Confirms Completion

CSM notifies the customer that the warm-up is finished and they can now use EDM in Journey normally. _Note: If the spam rate is too high during the warm-up, the customer must clean their contact list, and the process reverts to Step 7._
